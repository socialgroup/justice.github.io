SOCIAL EVENT ON 24 FEBRUARY 2017

In its efforts to contribute to a good relationship between refugees and Hong Kong community, Social Justice Alliance (SJA) has organized Potluck on 24 February 2017. The event will be hosted within the premises of The GoodLab. Every envitee will bring food to share together with others. This social event will be an occasion for exchanging ideas, learning from each other and supporting refugees in Hong Kong.

ADDRESS:The Sparkle, 500 Tung Chau Street, Cheung Sha Wan
