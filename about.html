Our Mission and Vision

Social Justice Alliance mission is to create a positive image of the refugee community by: engaging in activities intended to change public perceptions; supporting new arrivals; and creating a platform to discuss challenges facing refugees in order to find adequate solutions.

We have a dream of enjoying equal rights and opportunities, integrating and contributing to a good relationship between refugees and the Hong Kong community.

Together we will create a positive image!
