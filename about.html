About LetsArt

LetsArt is an association of men and women who tried find solutions of their problems and came up with the idea of meeting regularly every week to do activities of drawing, painting, learning languages, making jewellies...currently this association is made of 20 members but we are inviting anyone who wishes to come and join us .

We are conscient of many benefits that drawing and painting can have on our health, as many of us need really to benefit given our condition of life which do not allow people to work.

Together we will fight the negative thoughts and feelings!
