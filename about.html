Social Justice Alliance Mission and Vision

Engaging in activities to change public opinion

We are seeking to change the perception of refugees in Hong Kong. The Social Justice Alliance wants Hong Kong people to see us as contributing members of the community. In order to do this, we are seeking to collaborate with the Hong Kong people. We desire to build relationships and learn.

Support New Arrivals

We know that arriving in Hong Kong as a refugee is difficult. We are looking for ways to support and help new refugees understand and navigate Hong Kong. We know that being an urban refugee can be particularly challenging and we want to help ease these challenges for others when they arrive. We are currently working towards developing a strategy to support them.

Together we will create a positive image!
