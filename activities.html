Our Activities

After its creation in 2015, Social Justice Alliance has accomplished various activities including talks made to some universities, sharing culture with local people through cooking and dancing. Other activities include writing documents about refugees highlighting our problems and challenges and sending them to members of parliaments. We spread also our message through newspapers.

We will continue engaging in activities that change the public perception, focussing on activities that allow us to meet more people. Activities we are planning include: Handcraft, teaching languages, cooking, singing, inviting people to give talk to refugees, holding a multicultural exhibit and more.
