Welcome to Social Justice Alliance Website

Social Justice Alliance is a group of refugees from different background living in Hong Kong. This group was created to find solutions to some of the challenges they meet while living in the community where they are not allowed to work but rather living on meager assistance from the government. This group serves as a platform to discuss challenges they meet, and to engage in activities that change public perception on this social group.
